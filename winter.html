Welcome to the Winter Events Page

Check out some of the top winter events listed below!

Liwa Festival ▼
Price Range: Entry is free, but activity prices may vary

A 23-day celebration of Emirati culture in the stunning Al Dhafra desert, featuring music, art, and thrilling adventures. Held from December 13, 2024, to January 4, 2025, entry is free, though activity prices vary. A must-visit for cultural enthusiasts and adventure seekers.
Yas Winter Fest ▼
Price Range for Single Day Entry:
  • Children (ages 3-12): AED 25
    • Adults (13+): AED 35

Price Range for Season Pass:
  • Children (ages 3-12): AED 85
  • Adults: AED 110

Additional activities may have separate fees.

A magical 10-day winter festival with a snow park, carnival rides, festive markets, and live entertainment. Running from December 13–22, 2024, entry costs AED 25 for children (3-12) and AED 35 for adults (13+), with season passes available. Additional activities may have separate fees.
Disney on Ice ▼
Price Range: Ticket prices vary based on seating and venu; please check official ticketing platforms for current pricing.

A family-friendly spectacle where Disney’s beloved characters and stories come to life on ice. Perfect for all ages, ticket prices vary based on seating and venue. Check official ticketing platforms for the latest prices and show dates.
Miami Vibes ▼
Price Range: AED 10 for adults, AED 5 for children (ages unspecificed)

A vibrant winter event showcasing diverse food trucks, local businesses, souvenir shops, and unique activities like a rage room and art workshops. A lively, family-friendly destination blending food, shopping, and creativity.
MOTN (Mother of the Nation) Festival ▼
Price Range: General admission starts around AED 30; additional activities may have separate fees

A cultural extravaganza celebrating art, music, and cuisine with activities for all ages. General admission starts at AED 30, with additional charges for select activities. A perfect event for those looking to immerse themselves in local and international culture.
The Rage ▼
Price Range: AED 60 (+ AED 10 for park entry ticket)

A trendy urban pop-up event featuring cutting-edge streetwear fashion, live music, and immersive art installations. A dynamic and modern experience for fans of street culture and creativity.
Formula 1 ▼
Price Range: Depends on seating in stand, check official website for details

The Abu Dhabi Grand Prix is a world-class motorsport event at Yas Marina Circuit, combining thrilling races with glamorous afterparties. Ticket prices vary based on seating and packages, making it a premier event for motorsport enthusiasts and socialites alike.
